## 🌐 Executive Abstract
This repository houses the core dataset, theoretical framework schemas, and architectural blueprints for the **Global Biocultural & Ecological Exposure Forecast Engine**. 

Traditional biosecurity and syndemic mapping models rely on centralized, privacy-invasive data extraction pipelines. This engine introduces an industry-agnostic, **Zero-Knowledge, client-side architecture** that calculates multi-layer health exposure risks completely locally on user hardware. By intersecting spatial epidemiology, medical anthropology, and environmental psychology, the engine delivers highly contextualized, time-aware risk forecasting across 50+ countries without collecting a single byte of personally identifiable information (PII).

## 🎯 Strategic Alignment

This framework is structurally designed to fit seamlessly into diverse enterprise and institutional health portfolios:

### 🏥 Digital Health Ecosystems (e.g., TELUS Health)
* **Zero Liability Data Pipeline:** Mitigates PIPEDA/HIPAA regulatory constraints by performing client-side matching. Legally integrates with wearables, consumer health records, and workplace safety tools.
* **Clinical System Interoperability:** Uses FHIR-compliant data layers to output instant, non-stigmatized triage assets (e.g., encoded QR indicators) directly into primary care Electronic Medical Records (EMRs).

### 🇺🇳 Global Policy & Surveillance (e.g., WHO / CDC)
* **Eliminating Diagnostic Lag:** Bypasses traditional 7-to-21-day administrative reporting delays by tracking ecological readiness (plant taxonomy, vector thresholds) and socio-behavioral rhythms.
* **Cultural Epidemiology Integration:** Maps health-seeking behaviors, community gathering intervals, and culinary food taxonomies alongside standard pathogenic matrices.

### ✈️ TravelTech & InsurTech
* **Dynamic Underwriting Context:** Provides multi-variate risk scoring (Incubation-Matched Movement Analysis) to automate travel safety profiles, corporate continuity routing, and proactive health alerts.
