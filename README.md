### 🏗️ The Interoperable Bridge
> "The core innovation is the **Encapsulated Triage QR**. By translating environmental, culinary, and temporal data into anonymized clinical indicators, we bridge the gap between epidemiology and EMR workflows."

* **Zero-Knowledge:** 100% Client-side; no PII leaves the device.
* **FHIR Standard:** Ready for HL7-compliant clinical ingestion.
* **WCAG 2.1 AA:** Accessible diagnostic indicators.

---

### 🚀 Industry Value Propositions
* ✈️ **Travel:** Know the risk before you go—without collecting data.
* 🏥 **Healthcare:** Travel history for clinicians, privacy for patients.
* 🛡️ **Insurance:** Risk-based pricing without accessing medical records.
* 🌍 **Public Health:** Track outbreak origins without tracking people.
* 🚓 **Police:** Find missing persons by analyzing where, not who.
* 🎓 **Education:** Teaching spatial epidemiology without privacy violations.
